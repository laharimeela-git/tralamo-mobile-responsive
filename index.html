Featured Packages

Handpicked experiences for unforgettable journeys

4.8

Maldives - Bandos

Starts from ₹60,800 Per Person

In the middle of the Indian Ocean sits the island of Bandos. A picture perfect paradise hidden away from life's everyday stresses. The clear blue water, vibrant plant life and white sandy beaches create an immediate feeling of tranquility.

Package Inclusions

  • 3 Nights accommodation in Standard Beachfront Resort
  • Meals on Half Board basis
  • Return Speedboat Transfer

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival - Upon your arrival at Male International airport, you will be welcomed by the agent's representative and will be transferred to your hotel via an enthralling speedboat ride. Explore the resort & its indoor activities or take a stroll by the serene beach.

Day 2

Adventure - After enjoying a hearty breakfast, you can opt go for a dive session (on extra cost) or rest and laze around by beach in your resort. Enjoy a delicious meal and retreat for a relaxing overnight stay.

Day 3

Day at Leisure - Have a delicious breakfast and proceed to enjoy this leisure day. Head to the exotic beaches to delight in the water play or simply laze on the soft, powdery sand.

Day 4

Departure - Enjoy a breathtaking sunrise on the last morning before another delicious breakfast. Check out and you will be transferred to the Male Airport as per the flight timings.

4.9

Thailand-Bangkok-Pattaya

Starts from ₹15,999 Per Person

Thailand is an amalgamation of scenic, adventurous and fun holiday destination to visit with friends and family. Experience modern cities, surreal islands, picturesque beaches, fun filled adventures, and thrilling nightlife.

Package Inclusions

  • 2-Nights Stay at 3 star in Pattaya Hotel-Hotel Golden Beach (Superior) with IBF
  • 1-Night Stay at 3 star in Bangkok Hotel- Hotel Seasons Siam (Standard) with ABF
  • Return Airport Transfer From Don Mueang International Airport (DMK Airport)
  • Pattaya Hotel to Bangkok Hotel Transfer (PVT)
  • Sightseeing as per the itinerary

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival | Alcazar Show - Pick up from Don Mueang International Airport and drop off at Pattaya hotel. Explore the streets of Pattaya. In the evening go for the Alcazar show with professional performers dancing in stunning costumes.

Day 2

Gems Gallery | Coral Island Tour With Lunch - Have breakfast at the Pattaya hotel and go to gems gallery and then to the pier for a speedboat transfer to Koh Larn island. Relax on golden beaches or try watersports like snorkeling and scuba diving.

Day 3

Transfer to Bangkok | Bangkok City Tour - After breakfast at the Pattaya hotel, head for a road journey to the capital city. Enroute city tour. Evening free for leisure and shopping.

Day 4

Departure - Fly back home with amazing memories!

4.7

Singapore

Starts from ₹24,500 Per Person

Singapore includes different shades of experiences in very affordable prices. The melting pot of different cultures, cuisines and architectural styles; amazing amusement and wildlife parks; wonderful shopping avenues - truly a global city of Southeast Asia!

Package Inclusions

  • 03 Nights accommodation in Singapore at Value Hotel Thomson or similar
  • Meals on Breakfast basis at the Hotel
  • Return airport transfers
  • All tours and transfers on SIC basis
  • Sightseeing as per the below itinerary

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival Singapore - Once your flight lands, you will be greeted and escorted to the hotel. At night, be prepared for an exceptional Night Safari trip to encounter wild animals in their natural habitat when it is dark.

Day 2

Singapore Half Day City Tour | Sentosa - Explore places like Art Museum, Esplanade Theatre, Merlion Statue, Marina Bay, and more. Visit Sentosa Island for the spectacular Wings of Time show and SEA Aquarium with cable car ride.

Day 3

Universal Studios - Have breakfast and get ready for a thrilling day at Universal Studios. There are 24 attractions in the park. Click pictures with cartoon characters and explore various themes like Madagascar, Hollywood, Ancient Egypt.

Day 4

Singapore Departure Transfer - Grab your breakfast and proceed towards departure transfer to Airport for Journey back to Country.
